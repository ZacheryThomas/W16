Use the arrow keys to control the snake. Click inside the gray border to start a new game.
To play a game of local multiplayer snake, one player controls their snake using the arrow keys, and the other via WASD, where W is up, A is left, S is down, and D is right.
To play a game of online multiplayer snake, one player must be the host and the other the client. The host must provide the client with their ID, which the client must enter into the text box before clicking join online multiplayer.
